2. Skin Assessment and UV Imaging Technology

ArtiSkin uses a professional UV skin imaging device as part of the consultation process. Clients should understand that:

  • The device uses safe UV-A light (320–350nm) for general cosmetic assessment purposes
  • Results shown by the device are visual indicators only — they are not medical diagnoses

  • Descriptions such as "dehydration patterns," "visible pigmentation," or "UV-related changes" refer to visual observations made for the purpose of tailoring beauty treatments — not medical findings

Sessions using the imaging device last under 3 minutes. The device is safe for most skin types. If you have a diagnosed photosensitivity condition or are taking photosensitising medications, please inform Jeena before your appointment.
