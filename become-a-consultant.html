The Journey

From conversation to consulting — what the path looks like.

1

A real conversation with Jeena — no commitment required

Message Jeena on WhatsApp. Tell her you're interested in the consultant pathway. She'll have an honest conversation with you — about your background, your motivations, your availability, and whether she thinks this is the right fit for you right now. There's no pitch. No pressure. Just a direct conversation.

2

Your own ArtiSkin client experience first

Before training anyone, Jeena wants them to have experienced the full ArtiSkin process themselves — the consultation, the skin assessment, the personalised plan, the follow-up. You can't give something you haven't received. This session is the foundation for everything that follows.

3

Training — hands-on, not just theoretical

Jeena trains you in person across multiple sessions — the skin assessment device, skin science, product knowledge, consultation technique. You'll practice on real people, including Jeena. Training isn't complete until you're confident and Jeena is confident in you.

4

Your first clients — with Jeena alongside you

Your first few consultations won't be solo. Jeena and the team are present or available — reviewing your approach, giving real feedback, ensuring your clients receive the same standard they'd get from any ArtiSkin session. This is where your confidence actually builds.

5

Building your own ArtiSkin practice

Once you're operating independently, you'll have your own clients, your own growing reputation, and the ArtiSkin team as an ongoing resource. You will be part of something with real roots — a network that shares the same mission, the same standards, and the same honest approach to skin health that builds the kind of client relationships that last.
