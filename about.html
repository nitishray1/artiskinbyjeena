Jeena's Story

I started ArtiSkin because I was tired of people being sold things that didn't actually fit their skin.

Hi, I'm Jeena. I'm a skin assessment certified, Artistry-certified skin consultant based in Merrylands, western Sydney — and I come to you.

My interest in skin didn't start in a salon. It started when I noticed that the women around me — friends, family, people I cared about — were spending real money on skincare that wasn't working for them. Not because the products were bad, but because they didn't really know their skin. They were following trends, not science.

When I completed my skin assessment training, something shifted permanently. Here was a tool that could show people, in real time, exactly what was happening beneath the surface of their skin. Congestion they didn't know was there. Dehydration their mirror couldn't detect. Sun damage sitting quietly under what looked like clear skin.

Seeing that moment — when a client looks into the skin assessment device and truly sees their skin for the first time — that's why I do this. It changes how people take care of themselves.

I'm also Artistry certified, which means I understand skincare from a formulation level — what active ingredients actually do, why layering order matters, what your skin needs at different life stages. I bring that knowledge into every session, not as a lecture, but as a real conversation.

I come to your home because skincare should fit into your life, not the other way around. I work as part of a team of dedicated practitioners — trained and guided by experienced professionals — which means every session is backed by more than just what I know alone. I remember what you told me last time. I follow up. I care about results that actually happen.

How Jeena works

What every ArtiSkin session stands for.

🔬

Education before everything

Before any product or treatment is recommended, Jeena makes sure you understand your own skin — what type it is, what it actually needs, and why. You leave every session knowing more than when you arrived. That's the point.

🤝

Honest — even when it's not what you expect

If your skin doesn't need something, Jeena will tell you. If what you're already doing is working well, she'll say that too. Trust is built by telling the truth, not by making every visit feel urgent.

📋

Proper follow-through — not a one-off experience

Jeena checks in at 4 weeks. She remembers what you discussed. She adjusts what isn't working. Real skin results happen over time, with someone consistently paying attention — not just someone who does a nice facial and disappears.

Jeena's Skin Journey

This work started as something personal.

J
Jeena Founder · ArtiSkin

✏️ Jeena — replace this placeholder with your real story in your own voice.

For a long time, my relationship with my own skin was one of quiet frustration. I tried the products. I followed advice from people I trusted. I spent money I sometimes didn't really have on things that promised to fix what I was seeing in the mirror — and most of the time, they didn't deliver.

What nobody told me — what took me years and a proper education to discover — was that I had been treating the wrong problem. I didn't understand what was actually happening in my skin. I was guessing. And guessing, even with expensive products and the best intentions, is not a skincare routine. It's hope.

Training with the skin assessment device changed everything. The first time I saw my own skin under UV light — the dehydration I hadn't known was there, the congestion sitting quietly beneath the surface, the patterns that explained years of frustration in one two-minute analysis — I felt something shift. It wasn't just information. It was clarity. Evidence. The ability, for the first time, to make decisions about my skin based on what was actually there.

I started ArtiSkin because I kept meeting women who were where I had been. Spending and guessing and feeling quietly defeated by their own reflection. And I thought: what if they could see what I saw? What if someone sat down with them, showed them their skin as it really is, and built them a plan around that truth instead of a trend?

That's what every ArtiSkin session is. That moment. That shift. The one where you stop guessing and start understanding.
