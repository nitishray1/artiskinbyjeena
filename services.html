Skin Consultation

The Foundation

Initial Skin Consultation & Assessment

The starting point for any new client. Includes a lifestyle and skin history intake, full UV skin imaging, identification of your skin type and current conditions, and a personalised skincare regime recommendation. This is where everything begins — understanding your skin before touching it.

Starting from $80 45–60 minutes · Includes Skin Analysis

Facial Treatments

Essentials

Classic Hydration Facial

Deep cleanse, exfoliation, hydration mask, toner, and moisturiser. Suitable for all skin types. An excellent first facial for new clients — gentle, restorative, and immediately visible results.

Starting from $90 45–60 minutes
Brightening

Skin Renewal Facial

An exfoliation-focused treatment designed to remove built-up dead skin, encourage natural skin renewal, and brighten the complexion. Ideal if your skin looks dull or uneven-toned.

Starting from $100 45–60 minutes
Technology

Ultrasonic Vibration Facial

Uses ultrasonic technology to deep-cleanse, tone, and support the skin's natural renewal process. Particularly effective for clients with congestion, enlarged pores, or early signs of ageing.

Starting from $110 45–60 minutes
Advanced Cleansing

Dermasonic Facial

Advanced sonic cleansing and skin rejuvenation. A step beyond the Ultrasonic — deeper cleansing action, improved product penetration, and a firmer, more refined complexion after.

Starting from $120 60 minutes
Resurfacing

Mushroom Enzyme Peel with Red Light Therapy

A mushroom enzyme peel gently resurfaces and renews the skin, followed by red light therapy to support the skin's collagen renewal and help soothe the skin. Suited to clients looking to improve the appearance of uneven texture, dullness, and visible pigmentation.

Starting from $140 60–75 minutes
Hydration

HydraFacial-style Treatment

A multi-step treatment combining deep hydration, gentle extraction, and active infusion. Leaves skin visibly plumped, clear, and glowing. Suitable for most skin types and great before events.

Starting from $130 60 minutes
Anti-Ageing

Collagen Boosting Facial with Red Light Therapy

Targets visible signs of ageing — fine lines, sagging, and loss of firmness. A lifting and firming treatment is followed by red light therapy to support the skin's natural collagen renewal process.

Starting from $150 75–90 minutes

Ongoing Support

Personalised Routine

Personalised Skincare Regime

Based on your skin assessment, Jeena builds a daily morning and evening routine using products and steps specifically chosen for your skin type, current conditions, and goals. Not generic advice. Your routine.

Included with your Initial Consultation or available as a standalone service.

Accountability

Follow-up & 4-Week Check-ins

Real change takes consistency and adjustment. Jeena checks in with you at 4-week intervals, tracks your progress, and adjusts your routine as your skin responds — to seasons, stress, hormones, and life.

Available to all existing clients. Ask Jeena about follow-up scheduling when you book.
