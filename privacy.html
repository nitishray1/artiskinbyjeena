Privacy Policy

How ArtiSkin by Jeena collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Last updated: June 2025  ·  Effective: June 2025

ArtiSkin by Jeena ("ArtiSkin," "we," "us," "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with care and transparency. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

By using our website or booking a service, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. Please read it carefully.

1. Who We Are

ArtiSkin by Jeena is a mobile skincare consultation service based in Merrylands, New South Wales, Australia. We provide general beauty and wellness services including skin consultations, skin assessments, and facial treatments. We do not provide medical services.

For privacy enquiries, contact us at: hello@artiskinbyjeena.com.au

2. What Personal Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact information: name, phone number, email address, suburb/location
  • Skin and health information: skin type, skin concerns, known allergies, current skincare products in use, and relevant lifestyle information (sleep, diet, stress levels) — shared voluntarily during consultations
  • Appointment information: booking history, service preferences, session notes
  • Communications: messages sent via WhatsApp, email, or Instagram
  • Images: before/after photos taken with your explicit written consent only

We do not collect sensitive medical records, government identifiers, financial information, or any information without your knowledge and consent.

3. How We Collect Your Information

We collect personal information directly from you when you:

  • Contact us via WhatsApp, email, or social media
  • Book or attend a skin consultation or facial appointment
  • Complete an intake conversation or provide skin history information verbally or in writing
  • Provide consent for before/after photography
  • Sign up for any future newsletters or communications (if offered)

We do not collect personal information through cookies, tracking technologies, or third-party data brokers on this website.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information only for the following purposes:

  • To deliver the skin consultation or facial service you have booked
  • To personalise your skincare recommendations and follow-up plan
  • To contact you regarding your appointment or follow-up care
  • To maintain session notes for continuity of care across appointments
  • To display before/after results on our website or social media — only with your explicit prior written consent
  • To comply with our legal obligations

We will never sell your personal information to third parties. We will never use your information for unsolicited marketing without your permission.

5. Sensitive Information — Skin and Health Details

Information about your skin conditions, allergies, or health history is considered sensitive information under the Privacy Act. We collect this information only where it is necessary to provide safe and appropriate beauty and wellness services, and only with your consent. This information is handled with additional care and is not shared with any third party without your explicit consent.

6. Photography and Before/After Images

ArtiSkin only photographs clients with their explicit prior written consent. Consent forms will clearly state:

  • What photos will be taken
  • How images may be used (e.g., website, Instagram)
  • Your right to withdraw consent at any time
  • That images will not be used without a signed consent form

You may request removal of any previously consented images at any time by contacting us at hello@artiskinbyjeena.com.au.

7. Who We Share Your Information With

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service delivery: if a colleague or team member assists with your appointment, they will have access only to the information necessary for your service
  • Legal compliance: if required by law, court order, or government authority
  • Your consent: where you have specifically requested or authorised a disclosure

WhatsApp, Instagram, and email communications are subject to the respective privacy policies of those platforms. We recommend not sharing highly sensitive health information through social media messaging.

8. How We Store and Protect Your Information

Client information is stored securely and access is restricted to ArtiSkin team members who need it to deliver your service. We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, loss, unauthorised access, modification, and disclosure.

We do not store personal information longer than necessary. Session notes are retained to support continuity of care across appointments. You may request deletion of your records at any time.

9. Your Rights Under the Australian Privacy Principles

Under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct information that is inaccurate, incomplete, or out of date
  • Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Withdraw consent for the use of photographs or testimonials at any time
  • Make a complaint about how we handle your personal information

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@artiskinbyjeena.com.au. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Complaints

If you believe we have handled your personal information in breach of the Australian Privacy Principles, please contact us first so we can attempt to resolve your concern. If you are not satisfied with our response, you may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at www.oaic.gov.au or by calling 1300 363 992.

11. Website and Third-Party Links

This website may contain links to third-party websites (including Instagram, WhatsApp, and Google Maps). ArtiSkin is not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies independently.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of our services after any update constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

13. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, access requests, or complaints:

  • Email: hello@artiskinbyjeena.com.au
  • Instagram: @artiskin.jeena
  • Location: Merrylands, Sydney NSW 2160
Note: This Privacy Policy has been prepared to reflect ArtiSkin's current privacy practices and our good-faith efforts to comply with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth). We recommend seeking independent legal advice to confirm compliance with all applicable laws as your business grows.
