How it works

This is not a facial.
It's the beginning of understanding your skin.

Every other routine you've tried started with products. This one starts with truth.

01

A real conversation — not a form, not a script

Jeena sits with you and genuinely listens. Your lifestyle, your concerns, what you've tried, what you've been told, what hasn't worked. No rushing. No assumptions. This conversation shapes everything that follows.

02

You'll understand your skin before anything is recommended

Jeena walks you through your skin type, what it actually needs right now, and why certain things you've been doing may be quietly working against you. You'll leave knowing your skin — not just owning a product list.

03

Professional skin imaging — see what your skin is actually doing

Using professional UV imaging technology, Jeena shows you conditions completely invisible to the naked eye — dehydration patterns, congestion, pigmentation, and areas showing visible UV-related changes. You see your own skin in real time through a magnifying lens. Clients call this the moment everything clicks.

04

A personalised plan — built around your skin, not a trend

Your skincare regime, your facial recommendation, your follow-up schedule — built specifically around what Jeena found in your assessment. She checks in at 4 weeks, adjusts as your skin responds, and stays with you for the long term.

As I build my practice and reputation, your experience is especially important to me. My focus is to provide genuine care, attention to detail, and a welcoming service you can trust.
